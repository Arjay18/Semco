Catv & Internet

SEMCO offers Cable Tv and Internet Services.

Cable Tv provides a stable and reliable signal that is less susceptible to weather-related disruption. It offers a variety of channels (60 channels), which the whole family enjoys.

Cable Internet provides speed and reliability at an affordable price. it is still an excellent option for internet access of the average family, even with multiple devices, streaming, and gaming, and most of all, it is very significant for the student's virtual classes and online activities.

3. Both CATV and Internet - Php 3,500.00

Monthly Subscription Fee :

1. Internet and CATV - Php 1,600.00

3. Internet only - Php 1,200.00
